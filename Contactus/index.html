Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For WhatsApp messages, expect an even faster response within 30 minutes.
Do you provide free consultations?
Yes! We offer free consultations to discuss your laundry needs, recommend the best services, and provide accurate quotes for your specific requirements.
Can I schedule a pickup through this contact form?
While you can request a pickup through this form, we recommend using our dedicated booking page for the fastest service scheduling, or contact us directly via WhatsApp.
What information do you need for a custom quote?
For accurate quotes, we need to know: type of items, quantity, your location, preferred service level, and any special requirements. The more details you provide, the better we can serve you.
How do you handle complaints or service issues?
We take all complaints seriously and aim to resolve issues within 24 hours. Contact us immediately if you're not satisfied, and we'll make it right with a re-clean or refund.
